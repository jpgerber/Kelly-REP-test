Can I change how I view others?

Yes, and the best way is to stop expecting people to fit these patterns. The factors identified in this test reflect how you expect others to act. This means we can sometimes let people fit our ideas rather than let them be themselves.
If you want to change, let people surprise you. People won't always act in the same ways. Learning these differences and noticing people again, giving them a chance to be different, can help you see new things in others.
